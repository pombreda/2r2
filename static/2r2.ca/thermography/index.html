With a professional team which is worried of his work’s quality, 2R2 is proud to count on inspectors who are qualified level 1 thermograph, qualification recognized all over the world. The professional and attentive inspectors are present for you with flexible schedules permitting to meet yours needs. Thanks to the thermography, which is a building inspection method at the fine point of technology, 2R2 proposes to you a thermographic inspection for your property without harm.

We offer to you a detailed and colored report with infrared and optical pictures, which allows you to understand easily the problems encountered in your own property; as water or air infiltrations, design faults, and so on.
